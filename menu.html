Not sure what your looking for?

Meetings

For your morning meetings, picture a delightful spread featuring bagels, danishes, yogurts, and fruits—arranged for your guests to help themselves. Explore our breakfast buffets for more details.

If your meeting falls during lunchtime, we can craft an array of assorted sandwiches, wraps, salads, fruits, and desserts for your guests to choose from. Alternatively, if you prefer pre-selected individual meals, our lunch boxes might be the perfect fit.

Parties

Whether it's a birthday celebration or your annual holiday party, indulge your guests with a luxurious menu offering 2, 3, or 4-course meal options. For a more daytime affair, consider our brunch menu.

In addition, we present an array of themed buffets that are sure to make a statement at any occasion. This option is ideal for events with diverse guest preferences, allowing them to help themselves to a variety of choices.

Gatherings

Hosting a gathering without the need for full meals? Our offerings include a variety of drink options and open bar selections. Enhance your gathering with our party nibbles—tempting spreads featuring fruits, cheeses, nuts, or antipasti.

Alternatively, make an impression with our new luxury 10pc Hors D'Oeuvres menus, boasting Asian, American, and vegetarian options. Enjoy fresh food and drinks prepared on the spot that are certain to leave an impression on your guests.
