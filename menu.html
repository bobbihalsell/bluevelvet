Not sure what your looking for?

Meetings

For your morning meetings we can curate a delicous breakfast spread of bagels, danishes, yofurts and fruits, for which your guests can help themselves. click here to see our breakfast buffets.

Meeting at lunch? Well we can create a spread of assorted sandwiches, wraps, salads, fruits and desserts for your guests to choose from here. Or if you prefer to have individual meals chosen ahead of time, check out our lunch boxes.

Parties

Whether its a birthday party or your works yearly holiday party, we can create a Luxury menu for your guests to choose from to enjoy a 2, 3 or 4 course meal, View here. Or if youre looking for something more day-time, check out our brunch menu!

Additionally we offer a range of themed buffets bound to make a statement at any occasion, this is perfect for an event where there may be many different guests who want different options, as they are able to help themselves!

Gatherings

Having a gathering, but not looking for full meals? We offer a multitude of drinks options and open bar options. To accompany, we recommend also indulging in our party nibbles, delectable spreads of fruits, cheeses, nuts or antipasti.

Or make a statement with our new luxury 10pc Hors D'Oeuvres menus, with Asian, American and vegetarian options. Unlimited fresh food and drinks in a set time that is made as it goes. These small bites will no doubt impress your guests.
